👩‍💻 About Me

My research investigates how combined modelling methods can improve interoperability across heterogeneous systems. I am particularly interested in combining multi-level modelling and multi-view modelling to create flexible yet consistent representations of complex systems.

🎯 Research Vision

To design a modelling approach that improves both vertical and horizontal interoperability while maintaining consistency across shared model views.

🧩 Core Themes

Multi-level modelling, multi-view modelling, consistency management, digital twins, and model-driven engineering.

⚙️ Methods

Conceptual modelling, consistency management, collaborative modelling, and logic-based reasoning.

🔍 Research Interests

My work sits at the intersection of system modelling, interoperability, and consistency-aware modelling support.
